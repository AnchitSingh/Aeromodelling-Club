About Us

“The way to get started is to quit talking and begin doing.” ― Walt Disney

Aeromodelling is the art of designing, building and flying miniaturized aircrafts (powered or non-powered).One can build a model plane from any material like Paper, Balsa wood,Composite etc.

It is both a hobby and sport; the hobby aspect involves building and assembling model aircraft, and the sport part involves the flying.

To showcase their passion for flying the club members participate in various fares like techkriti, held at IIT Kanpur and similar shows held at other institutes all over the country.
